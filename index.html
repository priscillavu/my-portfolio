Cloud storage and content creating app developed to help business professionals and students manage files, deliver projects and collaborate with peers. BusyBus

Transit app designed to help frequent bus riders plan the most efficient route and catch the right bus at their select bus route. It shows users the next bus times, time remaining to arrive at the bus stop, and route info. Designed to save user commute time and prevent congested bus routes. Developed on Figma and built using HTML/CSS.
